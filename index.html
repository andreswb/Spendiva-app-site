Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new trip?

Go to the Trips tab and tap the "+" button. Enter your destination, select the country/currency, set dates, choose the trip purpose, optionally add a trip nickname (up to 15 characters), and enter the exchange rate if needed. Your new trip will become active automatically.

How do I add an expense?

From the main screen, tap on a category (Meals, Transport, Stay, Fun, Coffee, or Shopping). Enter the amount using the numerical keyboard, select how many people are splitting the cost (1-4 free, 5+ requires Pro), choose the payment method (Personal or Corporate - Corporate requires Pro), select the date (Today, Yesterday, or Pick Date), and tap Add.

How do I add subcategories to expenses?

Long-press (hold) on any category icon on the main screen to open the subcategory picker. Select a subcategory (like "Restaurant" for Meals or "Taxi" for Transport), then proceed to enter the amount. Note: Subcategories are fixed for each category and cannot be customized.

Can I add expenses in different currencies?

With Spendiva Pro, you can enter an expense in any currency that differs from your trip currency. The app will automatically convert it to your trip currency and display both the original amount and the converted amount. This feature requires Spendiva Pro.

What's the difference between Personal and Corporate payment methods?

Personal expenses are tracked with your own money (free). Corporate expenses are for business travel reimbursement (requires Spendiva Pro). The PDF reports show payment method breakdowns to help with corporate expense reporting.

How do I split costs with more than 4 people?

When entering an expense, tap the "+" button after the 4-person quick select. This allows you to enter a custom number up to 100 people. Splitting with 5 or more people requires Spendiva Pro.

Can I add past dates?

Yes! When entering an expense, use the date selector above the keyboard. Choose "Yesterday" for quick access or "Pick Date" to select any date and time from the calendar.

How do I edit or delete an expense?

Tap the daily total at the top of the main screen to view all expenses. Swipe left on any expense and tap "Details" to edit the amount, date, category, subcategory, payment method, number of people, or add notes (up to 25 characters). Within the details screen, you can also delete the expense.

How do currency conversions work?

When creating a trip, enter the exchange rate (how many local currency units equal 1 unit of your home currency). All expenses are tracked in the local currency and automatically converted for reports. Note: Exchange rates are approximate estimates based on historical data (January 2025) and should be verified for accuracy.

Can I manage multiple trips at once?

Yes! Go to the Trips tab to see all your trips. Tap any trip to make it active. The main expense screen will always show expenses for your currently active trip. You can have multiple trips in different date ranges.

What are trip nicknames?

Trip nicknames are optional short identifiers (up to 15 characters) that help you distinguish similar trips. For example, "Summer Trip" or "Q1 Business." They appear alongside the destination in trip lists.

How do I view spending reports?

Tap the chart icon at the top right of the main screen. You'll see a complete trip summary with total spent, per capita costs, daily average, payment method breakdown, and detailed category breakdowns. Use the segment control to switch between Daily, Weekly, and Monthly views.

How do I export my expense data?

With Spendiva Pro, go to Settings and choose either "Export Current Trip (PDF/CSV)" or "Export All Trips (PDF/CSV)". PDF reports include charts and payment method breakdowns. CSV files can be opened in Excel, Numbers, or Google Sheets for further analysis. Export features require Spendiva Pro.

What features does Spendiva Pro include?

Spendiva Pro is a one-time purchase with lifetime access that includes: Multi-currency expense entry, unlimited expense splitting (5+ people), corporate payment method tracking, professional PDF reports, and CSV export.

How do I change the app language?

Go to Settings and select your preferred language (English, Espa√±ol, or Deutsch). The app will immediately switch to the selected language, including all categories, reports, and interface elements.

How do I archive a trip?

In Settings under "Current Trip," tap "End & Archive Trip". You can also swipe left on any trip in the Trips tab. Archived trips are hidden from the main list but can be viewed in the archived section. All expense data is preserved.

Can I add notes to expenses?

Yes! View your expenses by tapping the daily total, swipe left on any expense, tap "Details", and add or edit notes in the note field (maximum 25 characters). Notes help you remember details like restaurant names or taxi destinations.

Is my data stored in the cloud?

No. All your data is stored locally on your device. Spendiva doesn't sync to any cloud service, which means your financial data stays private and secure on your device. You can export data manually if needed.

Is Spendiva optimized for iPad?

Yes! Spendiva includes iPad-specific layouts with split-screen views, larger displays for expense lists and reports, and grid layouts optimized for larger screens.
